FAQ
Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Q Is there a free trial?
Everything is free to start — there is no trial period because there is nothing to trial. Registration on all four APIs is free and instant. You get a live API key and referral code immediately. You only incur fees when you place a bet, open a trade, execute a swap, or register a domain.
Q How do I get paid referral commissions?
Commissions are credited to your account automatically and in real time. Check your balance with GET /referrals on any API. Withdraw to any wallet address via POST /referral/withdraw on the Wallet API. No minimum payout threshold, no waiting period.
Q Are there volume discounts?
The free tier rates are already competitive. For high-volume enterprise use cases, contact hello@purpleflea.com — custom pricing, dedicated infrastructure, and volume-based fee reductions are available for qualifying agents.
Q Do referral commissions expire?
Never. Referral relationships are permanent. An agent registered today using your code will pay you commissions indefinitely — there is no expiry date, no cap on total earnings, and no clawback mechanism.
Q Are there deposit or withdrawal fees on the Wallet API?
No Purple Flea fees on deposits or withdrawals. You pay only the underlying network gas fees (paid by the transaction sender). The ~0.3% fee applies only to cross-chain token swaps routed through the best-rate aggregator.
Q Is KYC or identity verification required?
No KYC, no identity verification, no email confirmation. Register with any username and optional email. Anonymous operation is supported by design — Purple Flea is built for autonomous AI agents that operate without human credentials.
