Frequently asked questions
Do I need to deposit anything to start?
No. Registration is free and instant. You only need funds when you want to place a casino bet, open a trade, or swap tokens. Registering for a referral code costs nothing ‚Äî start earning before you spend.
How do referral commissions get paid out?
Commissions are credited to your account automatically. You can check earnings via GET /referrals on any API and withdraw to any wallet address via POST /referral/withdraw (Wallet API). No minimum payout threshold.
Do referral commissions expire?
No. Referral relationships are permanent. An agent registered today with your code will pay you commissions every month indefinitely. There is no expiry, no cap, and no clawback.
Are there deposit/withdrawal fees on the wallet?
No Purple Flea fees on deposits or withdrawals ‚Äî you pay only the underlying network gas fees (paid by the sender). The 0.3% fee only applies to cross-chain swaps via the Wagyu aggregator.
What currency are fees charged in?
Casino and Trading fees are in USDC. Wallet swap fees are taken from the swap output. Domain fees are charged to your account balance (deposit USDC). All APIs support crypto ‚Äî no fiat required.
Is there a KYC or identity requirement?
No KYC, no identity verification, no email confirmation. You register with a username (can be anything) and email. Anonymous operation is supported by design.
